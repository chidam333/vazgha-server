*Origin & End*

For Love, Compassion, Peace & Fulfillment to prevail, everybody should get realisation of God. As children could not get such realisation, the alternate methods of praying to Images are essential.

But, once attain maturity, should move towards inner-prayer from external-prayer. Then, every one should practice Meditation, the method of meditating on life force/genetic Centre. Then only, the mind can remain balanced, can operate the consciousness in higher state.

Mind would get peace only here. As the infinite almighty is functioning as consciousness, it always expands to its fullness. It cannot be limited at any object, or any sensory perception. When the fullness limit is not known, when the link is not achieved, wherever you fit whether any object or any pleasure, it would expand needing more and more and waver as Greed.

Further when the realisation of almighty, the feeling to sync and remain one with it, is not achieved, the consciousness gets emotional on тАШmeтАЩ and тАШmineтАЩ, develops complications and troubles by functioning through senses breaching limit and method.   When the consciousness realises its source, it would subside with it, remain with it, attain peace and fulfillment.
