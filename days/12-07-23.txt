*Daily A Noble Thought - July 12*

*Objective of Organisation*

At earlier days as there was less opportunity for education, the vocational opportunity only through parents, the four stages of youth, family, meditation and perfection i.e., Bhakti yoga, Karma yoga, Gnana yoga and Raja yoga were functioning separately. Now, the opportunity of education has increased significantly. It is so happening now that through scientific equipments and machines, anybody can perform any profession. For those who have not grown in age and knowledge, Bhakti Yoga (Order of devotion) is must. But, once moved to the stage of family, the Raja yoga and the Gnana yoga should also be linked step by step.

Like the authority of

ЁЯФ║ Vysya (trader),
ЁЯФ║ Kshatriya (ruler) and
ЁЯФ║ Brahmana (the realised)

are linked in the life of family personnel now, the Bhakti yoga, Karma yoga, Raja yoga and Gnana Yoga should also be linked with people who lead life in family. That means, the Raja yoga of SKY Yoga (Simplified Kundalini Yoga ~Art of enriching mind) and Gnana yoga of wisdom realisation, should merge with life, an opportunity needs to be created now for such merger; should increase. Such need is overwhelmingly felt with many of those who lead family life now. With lack of opportunity to fulfill the need, the knowledge stagnation, itтАЩs diversion, creating ruckus in society and it goes on increasing.

The objective of World Community Service Centre (WCSC) is the duty to help all those who are in need of and who all are eligible, to practice Rajayoga in family life and learn Gnanayoga for self realisation, knowing the current state.
