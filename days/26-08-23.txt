*Daily A Noble Thought - August 26*

*Shivayogam*

If man gets the technique to observe and perceive the nature of pleasure and pain of other living beings, once perceived turning up to help compassionately, then the mind of man would attain the divine feeling of virtue. IsnтАЩt the compassion arise in the mind to remove the hardship of others? That is attachment. Such attachment, such true attachment while practicing with others, the attitude of service blossoms from it.

Realised the Almighty, Shown is love. Consciousness realised the state of God (Almighty); while functioning it blossomed as Love. When we look at it as тАШWhat is LoveтАЩ, it would be known that it is the тАШAct of GodтАЩ. It is the nature of God to come as a result for every action.

Hence, if each one understand, respect, discharge the duty with attachment with everyone that I would do only good things, itтАЩs Shivayogam. Can see God in any object. Can remain as God in any state. Can attach with God; can immerse with God. Even if apart by physical body, can realise by consciousness that its one with God and living beings. Only meditation and introspection can can lift the consciousness to such state.
