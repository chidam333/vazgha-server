*Daily A Noble Thought - May 25*

*Fulfilled Life*

тЭУ Question :  Swamiji says that the life shines on meditation. Can the destiny be changed by meditating?  Are all meditators leading fulfilled life?

ЁЯОп Answer :  Life can be made shining by meditation.  As an evidence, it is enough to quote the following couplet from Thirukural :

They who labor on, without fear and without fainting
Will see even fate (put) behind their back

Just remember the proverb that the knowledge can conquer the destiny. Knowledge is consciousness. By meditation, it gets strength and sharpness. This knowledge streamlines the actions to be performed, arranges in life the greatness and success, not achieved so far.

If the meditators do it in a continuous and proper manner, further perform the introspection exercise taught in SKY Centers (Centers for the Art of Enriching Mind) with due attention, they can attain sublimation to change their natural instincts. Finally, they can attain Perfection (realisation) too.  It is the same fulfilled life, what you have questioned. Many of our SKY center members have attained such great life.

*- Vethathiri Maharishi*
