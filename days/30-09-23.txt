*Daily a Noble Thought - Sep 30*

*Animal, Man & Superman*

Material life is observing the objects through the senses; while observing the bio-magnetism is spent and felt as pleasure, pain, calmness and ecstasy. Beyond this, man also has the mega power to understand wisdom, understand nature and lead a blissful life. But, if we assess whether other living beings have this consciousness? (The reply is) they too have. But they do not have the opportunity to apply this consciousness.

Once the hunger is felt, you should eat. But, can any living species make food for eating? In the world, only human has the capability to produce the food on its own. Other species do not have. What do they do?

тАШOther species should go into its mouth. Besides, it should be within its strength to kill and eatтАЩ, thus all species search their suitable food. All living beings in the world, except the first sensory plants, the second sensory insects, birds, animals etc., all live by killing other species. But we cannot blame these species that they murder, on this. Because, they do not know how to produce its food.

But, we take it as a crime when a man does that. Because we can think to avoid troubles and live happily. If this thought is brought into action, man has the knowledge to plan life accordingly. Thus, we can understand the speciality of humans. Man becomes Superman when he understands this speciality, sets the life not harming anybody and help others by spiritual service to lift the consciousness of those who live life miserably ignorant of this.

*- Vethathiri Maharishi*
